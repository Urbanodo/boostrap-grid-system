Amiwô (Red paste from Benin)

Amiwô is a red corn-based pasta that is very popular in Benin and throughout the sub-region.

It is usually served with a sauce commonly known in the Fon language as "Monyo," which is made with tomatoes and peppers.

Braised Chicken with Okra Sauce

Braised chicken with okra sauce is a classic African dish, beloved for its rich flavors and unique texture.

This dish is perfect for family meals or special occasions. Discover how to prepare this delicious dish, which perfectly combines the tenderness of chicken with the slightly viscous flavor of okra.

About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Dahlvirose Delights has quickly become a benchmark in gastronomy. Our award-winning chef, FIFA Elnette Zossou, combines traditional techniques with modernity to create unique dishes.

We are committed to using local and seasonal ingredients, working directly with producers in our region. Each dish is prepared with care and passion to deliver a memorable culinary experience.

Our restaurant has been recognized with several culinary awards and is regularly featured in prestigious dining guides.
